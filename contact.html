Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I purchase digital products? ▼

Select your desired product, choose a payment method (Card, PayPal, easypaisa, JazzCash), complete the payment, and you'll receive an instant download link via email.

Are the affiliate products shipped to Pakistan? ▼

Yes! Products on Daraz ship directly within Pakistan. Amazon products may have international shipping options. Check each product page for shipping details.

Can I get a refund on digital products? ▼

Due to the nature of digital products, we generally don't offer refunds. However, if you experience any issues, contact us and we'll work it out.

How can I collaborate with Kakakel? ▼

We're open to sponsorships, partnerships, and collaborations! Reach out via our contact form or email us at support@kakakel.com with your proposal.

Do you offer gaming setup consultations? ▼

Yes! Contact us with your budget and requirements, and we'll help you build the perfect gaming setup. This service is completely free.
